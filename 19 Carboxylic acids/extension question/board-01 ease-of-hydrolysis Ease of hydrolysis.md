# Ease of hydrolysis

1. Among chlorobenzene, chloroethane and ethanoyl chloride, what is the order for the ease of hydrolysis?
Answer: Chlorobenzene does not hydrolyse < chloroethane is slow < ethanoyl chloride is fast.

2. What are the two factors used to explain why acyl chloride hydrolyse more readily than a halogenoalkane?
Answer: electronic factor and steric factor
