# reactions

1. State the reagent and condition needed to convert an alcohol to an alkene.
Answer: concentrated H₃PO₄, heat

2. State the reagent and condition needed to convert phenol to 2,4,6-tribromophenol.
Answer: Br₂(aq).

3. State the reagent and condition needed to convert phenol to phenyl ethanoate.
Answer: CH₃COCl under anhydrous conditions.
