# reactions

1. What is the reagent and condition to convert an alcohol to an alkene?
Answer: concentrated H₃PO₄, heat

2. What is the reagent and conditon to convert phenol to 2,4,6-tribromophenol?
Answer: Br₂(aq).

3. What is the reagent and condition to convert phenol to phenyl ethanoate?
Answer: CH₃COCl under anhydrous conditions.
