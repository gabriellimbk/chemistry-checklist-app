# 1. Reactivity

1. How does a –CH₃ group affect the reactivity of benzene towards electrophilic attack?
Answer: The –CH₃ group is electron-donating, increasing the electron density of the benzene ring and making it more susceptible towards electrophilic attack.

2. How does a –NO₂ group affect the reactivity of benzene towards electrophilic attack?
Answer: The –NO₂ group is electron-withdrawing, decreasing the electron density of the benzene ring and making it less susceptible towards electrophilic attack.
