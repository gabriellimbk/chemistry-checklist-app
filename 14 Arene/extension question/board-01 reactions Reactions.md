# Reactions

1. State the reagents and conditions needed to convert benzene to nitrobenzene.
Answer: Concentrated HNO₃ and concentrated H₂SO₄ at 50 °C.

2. State the reagents and conditions needed to convert benzene to chlorobenzene.
Answer: Cl₂ with anhydrous AlCl₃. AlCl₃ acts as a Lewis acid catalyst to generate the Cl⁺ electrophile.

3. What reagents and conditions oxidise methylbenzene to benzoic acid?
Answer: KMnO₄(aq), H₂SO₄(aq), heat.
