# Balancing half equations

1. What are the three additional steps required to convert a balanced acidic half-equation into a basic medium half-equation?
Answer: 1. Add OH⁻ ions to both sides of the half-equation to neutralise all H⁺ ions.
  2. Combine H⁺ and OH⁻ on the same side to form H₂O.
  3. Re-balance the equation by eliminating any common terms, such as H₂O, from both sides.
