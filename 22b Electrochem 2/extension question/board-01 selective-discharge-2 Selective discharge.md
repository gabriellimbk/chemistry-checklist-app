# Selective discharge

1. What factors affect which species is preferentially oxidised or reduced during electrolysis?
Answer: E° value, state of electrolyte, ion concentration, and electrode material.

2. What species should be considered for oxidation at the anode?
Answer: Anions, electrode material, and neutral species.

3. What species should be considered for reduction at the cathode?
Answer: Cations and neutral species.
