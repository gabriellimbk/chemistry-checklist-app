# Selective discharge - How to deduce which species is preferentially [O] or [R]?

1. For oxidation at the anode, should the equation with the most positive E° value or most negative E° value be selected?
Answer: The equation with the most negative E° value.

2. For reduction at the cathode, should the equation with the most positive E° value or most negative E° value be selected?
Answer: The equation with the most positive E° value.
