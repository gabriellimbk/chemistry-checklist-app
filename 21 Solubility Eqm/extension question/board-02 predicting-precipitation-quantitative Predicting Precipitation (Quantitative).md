# Predicting Precipitation (Quantitative)

1. When predicting precipitation quantitatively, what comparison is made?
Answer: Compare ionic product, IP, with Ksp. Precipitation occurs when IP > Ksp.

2. After precipitation has occurred, what is the relationship between IP and Ksp in the remaining solution?
Answer: The remaining solution is saturated, so IP = Ksp.

3. What key phrases suggest that IP = Ksp should be applied?
Answer: solubility, saturated solution, before it begins to precipitate, and mixture after precipitation.
