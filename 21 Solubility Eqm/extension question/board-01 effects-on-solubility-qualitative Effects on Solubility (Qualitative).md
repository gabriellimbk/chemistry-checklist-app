# Effects on Solubility (Qualitative)

1. What happens to the solubility of ZnCO₃ when Zn(NO₃)₂ (s) is added to the solution?
Answer: Zn(NO₃)₂ increases [Zn²⁺], so the equilibrium shifts left and the solubility of ZnCO₃ decreases.

2. What is the effect of complex-ion formation on the solubility of a salt?
Answer: It results in increased solubility.
