# period - 3 - oxides - chemical - properties

1. What is the trend in bonding of Period 3 oxides and their acid-base nature?
Answer: Across the period, bonding of oxides changes from ionic to covalent and the nature of the oxides changes from basic to amphoteric to acidic.

2. What product forms when Al₂O₃ reacts with NaOH(aq)?
Answer: Na⁺[Al(OH)₄]⁻
