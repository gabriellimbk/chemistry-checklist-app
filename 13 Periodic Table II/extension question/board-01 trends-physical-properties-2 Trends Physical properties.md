# Trends: Physical properties

1. How does bond dissociation energy of X₂ change down Group 17?
Answer: Bond dissociation energy of X₂ decreases down the group.

2. How does volatility of X₂ change down Group 17?
Answer: Volatility of X₂ decreases down the group because size and polarisability of the electron cloud increase, strengthening instantaneous dipole-induced dipole interactions.
