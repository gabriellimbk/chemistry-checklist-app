# Carbonates: Thermal stability

1. How does the decomposition temperature of Group 2 carbonates change down the group?
Answer: The decomposition temperature increases down the group.

2. Why does thermal stability of Group 2 carbonates increase down the group?
Answer: Down the group, cationic radius increases, charge density and polarising power of the cation decrease, so the electron cloud of CO₃²⁻ is polarised to a smaller extent, there is less weakening of the covalent bond in CO₃²⁻ and hence more energy is required to break the covalent bonds for the decomposition.
