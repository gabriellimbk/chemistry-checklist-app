# VSEPR

1. What is the molecular geometry and bond angle of ClF₃, which has 3 bond pairs and 2 lone pairs around the central chlorine atom?
Answer: The molecular geometry is T-shape and the bond angle is 90°.

2. What is the molecular geometry and bond angle of the IF₂⁻ ion, which has 2 bond pairs and 3 lone pairs around the central iodine atom?
Answer: The molecular geometry is linear and the bond angle is 180°.
