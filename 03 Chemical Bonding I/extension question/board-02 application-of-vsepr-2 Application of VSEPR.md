# Application of VSEPR

1. State the fundamental principle of VSEPR theory regarding the spatial arrangement of electron pairs around a central atom?
Answer: Electron pairs (both bond pairs and lone pairs) around the central atom arrange themselves as far apart as possible in space to minimize mutual repulsion.

2. What is the relative order of repulsion strength between lone pairs (lp) and bond pairs (bp) of electrons according to VSEPR theory?
Answer: lone pair-lone pair (lp-lp) repulsion > lone pair-bond pair (lp-bp) repulsion > bond pair-bond pair (bp-bp) repulsion
