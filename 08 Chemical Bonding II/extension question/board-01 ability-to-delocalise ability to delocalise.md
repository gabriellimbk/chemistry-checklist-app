# ability to delocalise

1. In the molecule CH₂=CH-CH=NH, in which orbital does the lone pair of electrons on the nitrogen atom reside?
Answer: The lone pair of electrons on the nitrogen atom resides in an sp² hybridised orbital.

2. Explain why the lone pair of electrons on the nitrogen atom in CH₂=CH-CH=NH is unable to delocalise into the adjacent π system?
Answer: The lone pair is in an sp² orbital, which does not overlap continuously with the p-orbitals of the adjacent carbon atoms because it lies in a different plane (perpendicular to the π system).
