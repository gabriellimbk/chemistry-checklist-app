# Hybridisation & orbitals

1. Describe the types of orbital overlap that form the carbon-carbon double bond in ethene?
Answer: The double bond consists of a σ bond formed by the head-on overlap of sp² hybridised orbitals, and a π bond formed by the side-on overlap of unhybridised p orbitals.

2. How many unhybridised p orbitals remain on each carbon atom in ethyne, and what type of bonds do they form?
Answer: Two unhybridised p orbitals remain on each carbon atom, and they form two π bonds via side-on overlap.
