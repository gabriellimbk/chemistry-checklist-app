# Circle the atoms where electrons can be delocalised

1. State the condition required for the delocalisation of π electrons to occur across a system of atoms.
Answer: There must be continuous side-on overlap of p-orbitals over at least 3 adjacent atoms.

2. What structural features should you look for to identify if an atom has a p-orbital available for delocalisation?
Answer: Look for the presence of a π bond, a lone pair of electrons, or an incomplete octet on the atom.
