# Relative reactivities

1. What is the relative rate of nucleophilic substitution for R–I, R–Br and R–Cl?
Answer: R–I > R–Br > R–Cl.

2. How does C–X bond strength change from R–Cl to R–Br to R–I?
Answer: The C–X bond strength weakens from R–Cl to R–Br to R–I.

3. Why does nucleophilic substitution occur more rapidly for weaker C–X bonds?
Answer: The weaker the C–X bond, the more easily it breaks, so nucleophilic substitution occurs more rapidly.
