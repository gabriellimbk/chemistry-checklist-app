# reactions

1. State the reagents and conditions needed to convert chloroethane to ethene.
Answer: NaOH in ethanol, heat.

2. State the reagents and conditions needed to convert chloroethane to ethanol.
Answer: NaOH(aq), heat.

3. State the reagents and conditions needed to convert chloroethane to propanenitrile.
Answer: KCN in ethanol, heat.
