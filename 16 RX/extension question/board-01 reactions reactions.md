# reactions

1. What is the reagents and conditions to convert chloroethane to ethene?
Answer: NaOH in ethanol, heat.

2. What is the reagents and conditions to convert chloroethane to ethanol?
Answer: NaOH(aq), heat.

3. What is the reagents and conditions to convert chloroethane to propanenitrile?
Answer: KCN in ethanol, heat.
